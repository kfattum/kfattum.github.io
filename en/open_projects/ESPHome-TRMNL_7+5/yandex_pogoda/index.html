    Yandex Weather Widgets for Inker

    10 independent widgets that process raw data from the Yandex Weather API:

    • Temperature: Current temperature, water temperature in the nearest body of water, as well as daily maximum (↑) and nighttime minimum (↓).
    • Humidity: Percentage of air humidity.
    • Sun: Exact times of sunrise and sunset.
    • Wind: Direction, current speed, and gusts (e.g., NW, 4→9 m/s).
    • Condition: General weather conditions.
    • Precipitation: Analyzes the hourly forecast and reports in plain English when precipitation will begin or end. (Example: "Rain will begin within the hour from 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM" or "It will end tomorrow at 10:00 AM.").
